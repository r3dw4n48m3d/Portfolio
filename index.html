Services

I specialize in conducting both manual and automated penetration testing to identify and address security vulnerabilities

Penetration testing, often called a pen test or ethical hacking, is a security assessment method used to evaluate the security of a system or asset within a business or organization. Pen tests employ various methodologies to examine a company's assets, identify potential vulnerabilities, and verify their authenticity.

Web-App Penetration Testing

Conducts a comprehensive manual penetration test on the web application to ensure its security.

System Penetration Testing

Performs a thorough manual penetration test on Active Directory and system environments to enhance security and safeguard against potential threats, ensuring the protection of critical infrastructure and connected systems from future attacks

OSINT

Carries out a comprehensive OSINT penetration test to gather and analyze publicly available information, identifying potential security risks and vulnerabilities that could be exploited by malicious actors, thereby enhancing overall security posture.
